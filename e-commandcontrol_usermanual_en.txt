	1
	Login screen
Steps to Log In to the E-Command Control Application:
* Enter your registered mobile number or email address in the Username field. 
* Enter your password in the Password field.
* Click the Login button.
* Upon successful authentication, you will be redirected to the dashboard. The available menu options will be displayed based on your assigned user privileges.
	  

	2
	Sign up page
Steps to Sign Up for the E-Command Control Application:
* Click the "Sign Up" button located below the "Sign In" option on the login page.
* Complete the sign-up form by entering the required information.
* Review and agree to the Terms & Conditions by checking the corresponding box, then click "Verify".
* An OTP (One-Time Password) prompt will appear on the screen.
* Access the email account provided during registration. An OTP will be sent to your email.
* Copy the OTP from the email and enter it in the designated field on the application.
* After entering the correct OTP, your account will be successfully created and ready for use.


	  

	3
	Forgot password
            Follow the steps to Forget password 
* On the login page, click the "Forgot Password" link if you are unable to recall your password.
* Enter the registered email address or phone number associated with your account and click "Confirm." 
* A prompt will appear requesting a One-Time Password (OTP).
* Check your email inbox (used during sign-up) for the OTP.
* Copy the OTP from your email and enter it into the prompt on the screen.
* Click "Confirm" to proceed. The system will automatically validate the OTP.
* Upon successful verification, you will be redirected to your dashboard.
